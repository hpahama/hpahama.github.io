Vocabulary Page

  • 3NF Compliance
    Ensuring database tables are in Third Normal Form to reduce redundancy and improve data integrity, unless justified otherwise.
  • About the Datasets
    Information detailing the datasets used in the project, including their sources and characteristics.
  • Brainstorming Process
    The initial phase where various datasets and project ideas were considered to determine the most suitable approach.
  • Clear Identifiers
    Using concise and consistent names for tables and columns to ensure clarity and avoid confusion.
  • Constraints
    Defining rules such as CHECK, NOT NULL, and UNIQUE to enforce data integrity and prevent invalid data entries.
  • Correlation Analysis
    A statistical method used to study the strength and direction of relationships between different variables.
  • Dataset Compatibility
    Assessing how well different datasets can be integrated and used together in the project.
  • Dataset Information
    Specific details about each dataset used in the project, including variables, sources, and usability.
  • Demographic Factors
    Characteristics of the population being studied, such as age, gender, education, and employment status.
  • Diagram
    A visual representation of the database structure, showing the relationships between tables and data flow.
  • Economic Indicators
    Metrics that signify economic performance, such as Gross Domestic Product (GDP), unemployment rate, and inflation.
  • Geography
    Data related to physical and regional aspects influencing the project, such as location, climate, and terrain.
  • Global Happiness Report
    The project's main topic, focusing on various metrics that measure happiness and well-being across different countries.
  • Indexes
    Database indexes created on frequently searched columns to enhance query performance and retrieval speed.
  • Learning Objective
    The goals or skills intended to be achieved through completing the project.
  • Mental Health
    The condition of someone's psychological and emotional well-being, a key focus area in the project.
  • Normalization
    The process of organizing data in a database to reduce redundancy and improve data integrity through successive normal forms.
  • Problem Sets and Solutions
    Challenges identified during the project and their respective solutions, presented systematically.
  • Referential Integrity
    Ensuring all foreign keys in a database correctly and consistently point to valid primary key values to maintain data accuracy.
  • Regression Analysis
    A statistical process for estimating the relationships among variables, often used to understand the impact of one variable on another.
  • Relationships
    Connections between different data tables in a database, usually indicated using foreign keys.
  • Statistical Software
    Tools used for statistical analysis, such as R, Python (Pandas), or SPSS, to analyze and visualize data.
  • Step-by-Step Plan
    A detailed plan outlining the steps to achieve the project objectives, including database design and data analysis methods.
  • Urbanization
    The process by which cities grow and higher population densities are achieved, often impacting various social and economic factors.
